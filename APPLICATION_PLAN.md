# FounderFlow AI - Application Plan

## Overview
FounderFlow AI is a specialized sales strategist for startup founders, acting as an autonomous research and outreach arm. It leverages AI to find, enrich, and engage B2B leads.

## Core Features
1. **AI Lead Finder**: Targeted company search based on industry, location, and size.
2. **Lead Enrichment**: Deep insights including pain points, company summaries, and prospect reasoning.
3. **Outreach Generator**: AI-powered personalized drafts for Email, LinkedIn, and follow-ups.
4. **Lead Scoring**: Automated 1-10 scoring based on lead quality.
5. **Next Action Suggestions**: AI-driven sales step recommendations.
6. **Lead Pipeline Management**: Track leads through customizable stages.
7. **Growth Analytics**: Visual dashboard for performance tracking.
8. **CRM Export**: Export functionality for external tool integration.
